Frequently Asked Questions

Questions beforebuilding the system.

Clear answers on how Simono works, what the engagement includes and when outbound starts producing useful signals.

The system
What does Simono actually build?
Simono designs and operates the complete outbound system: positioning, ICP definition, prospect data, email infrastructure, messaging, sequences, CRM workflows, qualification and reporting. The objective is not simply to send more outreach, but to create a repeatable source of relevant commercial conversations.
Do you work as an outsourced SDR team?
Not exactly. Simono is a systems partner. Outreach execution can be managed as part of the engagement, but the core value is the infrastructure, process and feedback loop that make outbound repeatable and measurable rather than dependent on one person.
Timeline & results
How long does it take to launch an outbound system?
A typical launch takes two to four weeks, depending on the existing infrastructure, the complexity of the market and the number of channels involved. This setup period protects deliverability and ensures targeting and messaging are properly validated before volume increases.
When should we expect to see results?
Early replies and market signals can appear during the first weeks after launch. A reliable view of performance normally develops over several cycles of testing and optimisation. Simono focuses on qualified conversations and system improvement rather than short-term activity metrics.
Working together
Which companies are the best fit for Simono?
Simono is designed for B2B companies selling high-value services or software, particularly recruitment firms, IT consultancies, SaaS companies and specialist service businesses that need a more predictable way to reach decision-makers.
What do you need from our team?
The most important inputs are access to commercial context, a clear understanding of the offer and timely feedback on replies and meetings. Simono handles the system and execution, while your team provides the expertise needed to sharpen targeting and qualification.
